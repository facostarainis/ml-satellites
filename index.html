ML Satellites

Artificial Intelligence, Satellites and Journalism

This project is an introductory guide to the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and satellite imagery in journalistic pieces.

Here you will find the best examples on how to use Machine Learning (ML) and Deep Learning (DL) to find stories in satellite images, tips from experts, some of my own productions, possible story ideas to investigate and much more.

My hope is that it will serve as a starting point for those who want to explore these techniques, which have enormous potential in journalism.
